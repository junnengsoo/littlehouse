Please add your own images to this folder.
Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF

The application will randomly select an image to display in the popup.
For testing, you can download sample images from sources like Unsplash (https://unsplash.com) or use your own photos.

Example:
1. Download an image
2. Rename it to something like "popup-image.jpg"
3. Place it in this folder
4. Run the application

Note: Make sure your images are appropriate for your use case, as they will appear as popups on your screen.
